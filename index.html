About Hollow Sources

Hollow Sources is an informational site regarding to the video game 'Hollow Knight' made by Team Cherry. The site offers information about the game and it's game mechanics.

The site provides you with every know-how of the game, including characters, lore, map layout, and many more.

This site non-profit and is in compliance with the required output of class CSIT 1102 of University of San Carlos - Talamban Campus.
